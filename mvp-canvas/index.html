What is Command Canvas?

The MagnaRix Command Canvas offers a unified view of your strategic goals, risks, and key performance indicators. Every element is drag-and-drop enabled so you can quickly adapt your plan as conditions change.

Why It Matters

Aligning strategic objectives, identifying potential obstacles, and measuring success metrics all on one board boosts transparency and accelerates decision-making. Investors gain confidence when they see a clear roadmap.

What You Get

By visualizing high-level goals alongside associated risks and KPIs, you enhance accountability and focus. Financially, this translates into reduced planning waste, faster pivots, and higher ROI.

How to Use

Simply drag initiative cards into “Goals,” “Risks,” or “KPIs.” Use the Timeline page to set quarters, assign dependencies, and simulate outcomes. Collaborate with your team to keep everyone aligned.
