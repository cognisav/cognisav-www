Command Canvas

Command Canvas is designed for C-suite executives, VPs, and Board members who demand instant insight into strategic objectives. By consolidating your Goals, Risks, and Key Performance Indicators onto a single interactive canvas, you’ll be able to:

  • Align Stakeholders across departments—everyone sees the same priorities, dependencies, and progress.
  • Identify & Mitigate Risks early—tie each Risk back to the exact Goal it could impact, so you never overlook a critical dependency.
  • Track Success with KPIs that dynamically highlight how each indicator measures against a specific Goal—no more siloed spreadsheets.
  • Export & Share your entire canvas as JSON for board decks, financial models, or due diligence packages with a single click.
Not sure where to start?

Goals ℹ️ Explain more →

Goals are the high-level, strategic objectives that you want to achieve. For example: “Expand into two new markets by Q4” or “Increase annual recurring revenue by 25%.” Each Goal should be clear, measurable, and time-bound.

Risks ℹ️ Explain more →

Risks capture the uncertainties or obstacles that could derail your Goals. Each Risk should follow the format: “If [condition], then [negative outcome].” For example: “If our vendor delays shipping, then market launch is delayed.”

KPIs ℹ️ Explain more →

KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) are the measurable metrics that tell you if you’re on track. Tie each KPI to a Goal—for example: “Monthly Active Users” for growth or “Net Promoter Score” for customer satisfaction.
